# Claim Boundary — A10 Core Foundation

## Allowed claims

- A10 is presented as an AI-assisted independent research framework.
- A10 can be described as a structured-prior / geometric-steering approach.
- A10 may be useful as a design language for constrained model construction and evaluation.
- Domain-specific claims require domain-specific validation.

## Forbidden claims

- A10 is a proven fundamental law.
- A10 is a universal theory of nature.
- A10 applications are automatically valid.
- A10 has been commercially validated.
- A10 has passed safety certification.
- A10 results are independent of AI assistance.
- A10 outputs should be accepted without expert review.

## Standard wording

> A10 is an AI-assisted structured-prior research framework. It is not a validated universal law or a completed industrial technology. Domain-specific claims require independent validation.
